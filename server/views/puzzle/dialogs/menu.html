Rules

» Select a piece by clicking, then click another piece and they will swap.
» Pieces will not swap if they don't have the same shape.
» The piece can not be selected if it's already on the right place or locked.
» Locked piece will be automatically released after 20 seconds.

Legend

» Red piece - locked by another player.
» Dim piece - isn't on its right place.
» Blue piece - selected by you.
» - loading.

    The unusual jigsaw puzzle you can play with your friends online. The difference form the usual jigsaw puzzle is that all pieces are initially collected but they are set to the wrong place. Swap pieces to set them to theirs right place.
